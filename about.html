ABOUT PENNYJUICE

Learn more about our mission and our founder for PennyJuice!

Dr. Emma Chen

At PennyJuice, we believe that natural, healthy ingredients are the key to creating refreshing, delicious drinks that everyone can enjoy. Our story begins in Las Vegas, where our founder, Dr. Emma Chen, combined her passion for healthy living with a desire to bring nutritious, tasty beverages to families. Driven by the goal of offering juices that are not only flavorful but also beneficial, Dr. Chen set out to create a company where quality and well-being come first.

From the very beginning, PennyJuice has focused on using only the finest, organic ingredients. Our juices are crafted with care and are always Non-GMO, free from preservatives, and made from the freshest fruits. We take pride in knowing that each bottle is packed with pure, natural goodness. Every step of our process, from sourcing to bottling, is designed to maintain the integrity and freshness of our ingredients, ensuring that you and your family enjoy the best nature has to offer.

At PennyJuice, we are more than just a juice company. We are a community dedicated to health, taste, and family. Join us in celebrating the power of natural ingredients, and let PennyJuice bring a little more freshness, flavor, and well-being into your life.
