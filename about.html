ABOUT PENNYJUICE

Learn more about our mission and our founder for PennyJuice!

At PennyJuice, we believe in the power of natural, healthy ingredients to make refreshing, delicious drinks for everyone. Founded with the mission to bring nutritious, tasty juices to the families of Las Vegas, PennyJuice uses only the finest, organic ingredients. Our founder, Dr. Emma Chen, started this company with a passion for healthy living and a desire to offer juices that are both good for you and great tasting!

Our juices are Non-GMO, free from preservatives, and made from the freshest fruits. Whether you're enjoying a glass with your morning breakfast or quenching your thirst on a hot day, PennyJuice is here to provide you with a refreshing, flavorful experience.
